FAQs

Can I surrender my pet for adoption?

We currently do not accept pet surrenders. Instead, we encourage responsible rehoming by finding a loving new home through trusted networks and pet adoption communities.

Are all pets vaccinated before adoption?

Yes! All our pets receive necessary vaccinations and vet checkups before they are cleared for adoption.

Can I adopt more than one pet?

Yes, but we assess each adoption to ensure you can provide the best care and environment for multiple pets.
